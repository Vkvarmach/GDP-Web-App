About Project


This is a Admin website which can displays and manages Smart-Mobility-iOS application riders information , bikes information , riders history , locations and can able to interact riders by sending email notifications.

By placing rental bikes in specified parking perimeters, the Smart-Mobility-iOS Application aims to provide a commuter facility. When using an iOS application for the first time, a user must register before they can log in. After choosing their source and destination, they must scan the QR code on the bike to begin their ride. Once they have arrived at their destination, they must end it.The ride price will appear based on the time needed to get there. .
